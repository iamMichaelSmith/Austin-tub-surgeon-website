Warranty Information

Industry-leading warranty coverage

We stand behind our refinishing work with clear, written coverage that protects your investment.

Coverage highlights

Residential bathtubs & tile: 5-year warranty against delamination, cracking, or peeling caused by workmanship.

Sink refinishing: 2-year warranty.

Commercial or rental refinishing: 1-year warranty.

If a covered issue appears within the warranty period, we’ll provide labor and materials for touch-ups at no extra charge.

Not covered

  • Damage from suction-cup bathmats, soap dishes, or items left on the surface.
  • Use of abrasive cleaners, acids, or products not designed for acrylic surfaces.
  • Steel wool, sandpaper, or other abrasive tools.
  • Water exposure within the first 24 hours after refinishing.
  • Damage from tools, ladders, or heavy items dropped on the surface.
  • Rust, corrosion, mineral buildup, or normal wear and tear.
  • Dust particles settling during dry time or damage from removing masking too soon.
  • Warranty is non-transferable.
