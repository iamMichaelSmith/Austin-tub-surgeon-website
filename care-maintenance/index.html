Care & Maintenance

Keep your refinished surfaces looking new

A few simple habits will protect your finish and extend the life of your refinished tub, tile, or sink.

Do this

  • Wait 24 hours before using the surface after refinishing.
  • Clean weekly with a mild liquid cleaner and a soft sponge.
  • Rinse after use and wipe dry to prevent mineral buildup.
  • Use a non-slip mat that is not suction-based.
Recommended cleaners

Gentle dish soap, non-abrasive bathroom cleaner, or a cleaner approved for acrylic surfaces.

Avoid this

  • Abrasive powders, scouring pads, or steel wool.
  • Bleach, ammonia, acids, or harsh drain cleaners.
  • Suction-cup bathmats, soap dishes, or organizers.
  • Leaving bottles, razors, or metal cans on the surface.

Care FAQ

Can I use bath bombs or bath salts?

Occasional use is fine, but rinse the surface after each bath.

What about hair dye?

Rinse immediately after use to avoid staining the finish.

How often should I clean the surface?

Weekly cleaning is ideal for maintaining shine and preventing buildup.
